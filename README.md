## 👨‍💻 About me

Full Stack Engineer with a **backend focus**, specialized in building high-performance APIs and backend services in **Node.js** and **TypeScript** with **NestJS**, **Express**, and **Fastify**. Solid experience in REST API architecture, third-party and internal integrations, and scalable microservices — always with an eye on quality and scalability.

```yaml
name:      Mário Henrique Lino Pereira
role:      Senior Full Stack Engineer (Backend-focused)
location:  Brazil · 100% Remote
stack:     Node.js · TypeScript · NestJS · PostgreSQL · Kafka · AWS
mindset:   Clean Architecture · SOLID · Spec-Driven · Harness Engineering
```

- 🏗️ I design and evolve backend services with **NestJS / Express / Fastify** and scalable microservices
- 🧩 I refactor legacy MVC codebases into **Hexagonal Architecture** applying **SOLID** and **Clean Architecture**
- 📨 I build async pipelines, queues, and background jobs with **Kafka**, **RabbitMQ**, and **AWS SQS**
- 🗄️ I model and optimize **SQL & NoSQL** databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, Elasticsearch)
- 🧪 I practice **automated testing**, **code review**, and observability with OpenTelemetry & the ELK Stack
- 🤖 I work with **LLMs in production**, **Spec-Driven Development**, and **Harness Engineering**
